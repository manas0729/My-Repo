Information of IT

Information Technology (IT) is the use of computers, software, networks, and the internet to store, process, and share information.

It Fields

  1. Software Development – apps, websites, aur software banana

  2. Web Development – websites design aur develop karna

  3. Cyber Security – data aur systems ko hackers se protect karna

  4. Networking – computer networks aur internet systems manage karna

  5. Database Management – data store, organize aur manage karna

  6. Cloud Computing – online servers aur cloud services (AWS, Azure)

  7. Artificial Intelligence (AI) – smart systems aur machines banana

  8. Data Science – data analysis aur insights nikalna

  9. Mobile App Development – Android aur iOS apps banana

  10. IT Support / Help Desk – technical problems solve karna

  11. Game Development – computer aur mobile games banana

  12. DevOps – development aur operations ko automate karna
